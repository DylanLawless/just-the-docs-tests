# Nested lists

## Ordered list numbering ❌

1. Ordered List 1
    * Unordered List 1
        1. Ordered List 2
            * Unordered List 2
                * Unordered List 3

----

1. Ordered List 1
    1. Ordered List 2 - Item 1
        * Unordered List 1 - Item 1
        * Unordered List 1 - Item 2
    2. Ordered List 2 - Item 2

----

1. Ordered List 1 - Item 1
    1. Ordered List 2 - Item 1
        1. Ordered List 3 - Item 1
        2. Ordered List 3 - Item 2
    2. Ordered List 2 - Item 2
2. Ordered List 1 - Item 2

----

### Nesting task lists ❌

 - [ ] level 1 item (task)
    - [ ] level 2 item (task)
    - [ ] level 2 item (task)
 - [ ] level 1 item (task)
 - [ ] level 1 item (task)

### Nesting a ul in a task list ❌

 - [ ] level 1 item (task)
    - level 2 item (ul)
    - level 2 item (ul)
 - [ ] level 1 item (task)
 - [ ] level 1 item (task)

### Nesting a task list in a ul

 - level 1 item (ul)
    - [ ] level 2 item (task)
    - [ ] level 2 item (task)
 - level 1 item (ul)
 - level 1 item (ul)
